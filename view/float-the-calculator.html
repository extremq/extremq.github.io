The calculator

Meet float – a really specific calculator made using Python.
What does it do? Eh. That wasn't my point of interest when creating this tool, really. Let me explain.

To begin with, I am in the first year of university. For me, that means physics (notice the boldness – the reader should assume it's important). Well, every week we interpret experimental data and need to calculate two specific things: error and linear regression.
We were given a tool for that. It doesn't need to be fancy; after all, it should be able to do two simple tasks.

I was thinking that... until I noticed some issues.
